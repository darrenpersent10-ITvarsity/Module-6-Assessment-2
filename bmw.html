BMW SERIES

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, commonly referred to as BMW is a German multinational corporate manufactorer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles headcuarted in Munich, Bavaria.

The Corporation was founded in 1916 as a manufactorer of Aircraft Engines, which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945.

In 2017, BMW was the world's fourteenth largest producer of motor vehicles, with 2,279,503 vehicles produced. The Company has significant motorsport history, especially in touring cars, Formula 1, sports cars and the Isle of Man TT.
